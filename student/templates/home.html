SM COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY

S M College of Engineering & Technology, one of the prestigious technical institutions in North Malabar, was established in 2003. The engineering college is fully equipped to meet the rising demands for greater facilities in the scenario of higher education. With its emphasis on quality education augmented by exposure and training in other individual skills, the institute's focus is on creating individuals who are allround performers and true professionals. The institute is located in the historic city of Kozhikode, in the Malabar region of the state and well connected by air, rail and road routes to the rest of the country. SMCEK is proud of its meritorious students, highly qualified faculty, state of art of laboratories, infrastructure and reputed alumni. Over these years SMCEK is providing affordable technical education and remains to be the best choice among the brilliant students in the country.
